// 01 — Origin

Born at the Edge of Real & Digital

"Why does your digital self look generic when your real self never does?"

That question is where Augtrix started. Not in a boardroom. Not from a trend report. From the frustration of watching millions of talented, creative people show up online wearing the same factory-default identity — because nothing better existed at a price they could reach.

We started by designing what we personally wanted to see in the world: high-precision digital wearables that look like they were pulled from a sci-fi concept studio. Not cheap overlays. Not throwaway filters. Assets — engineered with the same obsession that goes into a bespoke jacket or a hand-finished watch, but made for the world you actually live in — screens, lenses, and all.

Drop 001 — the Predator Cybermask — took months of iteration. Six texture maps. Three export formats. 4K cinematic renders from every angle. A live Snapchat lens. A readme manual. That's what a first drop looks like when you build it like it matters.
