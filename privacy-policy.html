Data Encryption Security Protocol

// 01 / Information Retrieval Circuits

At Augtrix (augtrix.com), privacy is engineered directly into our architecture.

This protocol governs how we collect, monitor, and encrypt telemetry data and customer digital wallet metadata.

We strictly operate under end-to-end data minimization protocols, ensuring your premium digital footprint remains completely classified.

We only harvest the absolute minimum data required to execute your premium transactions and optimize your AR deployment experience:

  • Transaction Metadata: When you acquire an asset pack, we securely process your email address, billing location, and payment gateway confirmation tokens.
  • Device Diagnostics: To guarantee optimal 3D rendering and prevent asset crashes, our system may log basic non-identifiable telemetry (such as device type, browser model, and OS engine).
  • Strict Exclusion: We never store, see, or collect your raw credit card data, bank details, or digital wallet passwords. All payment processing is managed via globally certified, encrypted gateway pipelines.
// 02 / Data Encryption & Metadata Usage

Your structural data is never commercialized.

We use the collected data channels exclusively for:

  • Immediate Asset Deployment: Delivering your high-res textures, .OBJ, .FBX, and .GLB files instantly to your terminal or email inbox.
  • Optimization Loops: Vetting and upgrading our Snapchat Lenses, custom AR filters, and website interfaces for smooth functionality.
  • Zero-Spam Guardrail: Your email is strictly used for order receipts or major asset drops. We will never rent, leak, or sell your data matrix to third-party advertisers.
// 03 / The Privacy Security Architecture
  • End-to-End Encryption: All telemetry and communication pathways between your device and the Augtrix server frame are fully encrypted utilizing modern TLS/SSL security protocols.
  • Cookies & Tracking Pixels: We utilize minimal, functional cookies to maintain your shopping state and verify your identity during download sequences. You can disable cookies inside your browser framework at any time.
  • Right to Data Erasure: As an elite consumer, you hold absolute authority over your data. You can contact our secure network node at support@augtrix.com to request total erasure of your customer profile from our databases.
